What Is This?

Two tools. One seamless prospect experience.

LearnBenefits consists of two connected tools: a prospect-facing widget that guides potential clients through a benefits assessment, and an agent admin dashboard that lets you manage settings, leads, and marketing — all in one place.

💬
Widget

The Benefits Bot Widget

A conversational chat interface your prospects interact with. Collects their company info, benefits interests, books a Calendly appointment, and captures a signed consultation authorization — all in one smooth flow.

🛠
Admin

The Agent Admin Dashboard

Your control center. Customize branding, manage your Calendly link, view leads and appointments, send outreach messages, manage your prospect list, and export a configured version of the widget for any agency.

🔗
Connected

Settings Flow Through

When you save settings in the admin and export the widget, your branding, agent info, and Calendly link are baked directly into the widget file — ensuring every prospect on every device sees the right info.

💡

How they stay in sync

Both tools are hosted on GitHub Pages at channelos.github.io/LearnBenefits/. When you update settings in the admin and click Export & Download Widget, your settings are baked into a new index.html file. Upload that file to GitHub and every visitor instantly gets the updated widget — on any device, anywhere.

Widget Features

What makes the widget smart

🆕
Widget

ICHRA Smart Branch

If a prospect selects ICHRA in the benefits interest step, the widget automatically activates 4 additional questions specific to ICHRA — budget, employee count, timeline, and current challenges. Non-ICHRA prospects skip these entirely.

✅
Widget

Multi-Select Interests

Prospects can select as many benefits interests as apply — health, dental, vision, life, ICHRA, voluntary — using checkboxes with a "Select All" option. All selections are captured and sent to HubSpot.

📞
Widget

Phone Auto-Format

As the prospect types their phone number, it automatically formats to (555) 555-5555. No parentheses or dashes needed — the widget handles it in real time.

📅
Widget

Calendly Lock

The "continue" button on the booking step is disabled and grayed out until Calendly fires a real booking confirmation event. Prospects cannot skip or bypass the appointment — they must actually complete a booking.

✍
Widget

Canvas Signature

A digital signature pad lets prospects sign the Scope of Appointment (SOA) consultation authorization directly in the browser. The signature is captured and submitted with the lead data.

👤
Widget

Agent Contact on Done Screen

After signing, the "You're All Set" confirmation screen displays your name, direct phone number, and email — pulled from your admin settings — so prospects know exactly who to expect a call from.

Settings & Export

How to update settings and push them live

This is the most important workflow to understand. Changes saved in the admin only go live for all visitors when you export and re-upload the widget file.

1

Open the admin dashboard

Go to channelos.github.io/LearnBenefits/benefitsbot-admin.html and log in with your password. Always use this hosted URL — not a local file on your computer.

✅ Using the hosted URL ensures the admin and widget share the same domain, which is required for the Export feature to work.
2

Go to Settings and make your changes

Update any of the 10 fields: logo, primary color, accent color, agency name, agent name, phone, email, tagline, Calendly link, or widget link. Reference the Field Reference Guide on the right side of the Settings page.

3

Click "Save All Settings"

This saves your settings to the browser so the preview and marketing tools reflect your info immediately.

4

Click "Export & Download Widget"

This generates a customized index.html file with all your settings permanently baked in — Calendly link, logo, colors, agent info, everything. Your browser downloads it automatically.

✅ This is the key step. Without exporting and uploading, your settings won't appear for prospects on other devices.
5

Upload index.html to GitHub

Go to github.com/ChannelOS/LearnBenefits. Click on index.html in the file list, click the pencil (edit) icon, then click the three dots ⋯ menu and choose Delete file. Commit. Then click Add file → Upload files, upload your new index.html, and commit again.

⏳ GitHub Pages takes about 60 seconds to update after you commit. Refresh the widget link after a minute to see your changes.
Marketing Tools

Three ways to reach prospects

The Marketing section has three tabs. Every message requires you to click Add Widget Link before you can copy — this ensures you never accidentally send a message without your booking link.

📋
Templates Tab

Outreach Templates

6 pre-written messages (email, SMS, LinkedIn) with language matching the widget intro — "financially predictable, cost-controlled benefits." Enter a prospect's first name and company to personalize, then add your widget link and copy. Includes a Generic message that requires no name.

👥
Prospect List Tab

Prospect List

Upload a CSV (columns: Name, Company, Phone, Email). New uploads merge into your master list — no duplicates. Select a prospect from the dropdown, choose a message type, add widget link, copy. Every message sent is tracked with date, time, and type in the prospect table.

📱
Social Media Tab

Social Posts

Save your LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and X/Twitter profile URLs. Ready-made posts for each platform use the same benefits messaging. Add your widget link and copy the post — ready to paste directly into each platform.

Leads & Appointments

Where prospect data goes

Every time a prospect completes the widget flow, their information flows to multiple places simultaneously.

📄
Backend

Vercel Backend API

The widget sends lead data to your Vercel serverless backend the moment the prospect enters their email. This is the primary capture point — it works regardless of what device the prospect is on.

🌐
CRM

HubSpot CRM

Every lead is automatically upserted as a HubSpot contact with their company, role, interests, phone, and email. A timeline note is added with all the details from the assessment. No manual data entry needed.

📱
SMS

Calendly SMS Alerts

When a prospect books through Calendly, the Calendly webhook fires an SMS to your phone via Twilio — includes the prospect's name, email, event name, time (Central), and the join URL. Works 100% reliably regardless of the widget.

💡

Where to check your real leads

The admin dashboard's Leads and Appointments pages show leads captured in the same browser — useful for testing. Your real lead data lives in HubSpot, and your appointment alerts come via SMS from the Calendly webhook. Check those for production use.

Daily Workflow

How to use this day-to-day

1

Set up once — then it runs itself

Go to Settings, fill in all 10 fields, save, export the widget, upload to GitHub. That's your one-time setup. After that the widget is live at your link.

2

Send the widget link to prospects

Use the Marketing tab to copy a personalized message, add your widget link, and text or email it. Or drop it in social posts. The prospect clicks the link and goes through the bot on their own.

✅ Your widget link: channelos.github.io/LearnBenefits/
3

Get notified when they book

You receive an SMS the moment a prospect books through Calendly — their name, time, and meeting join link. No need to check the admin for appointments.

4

Check HubSpot for lead details

All their assessment answers — company, role, employee count, interests, ICHRA details — are in HubSpot as a contact with a timeline note. Review before the call.

5

Update settings anytime

Change your Calendly link, update your photo, new tagline? Go to Settings → update → Save → Export & Download Widget → upload to GitHub. Takes about 3 minutes.
