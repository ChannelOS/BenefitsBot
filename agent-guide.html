Overview

Your personal benefits assessment tool

The LearnBenefits Widget is a smart conversational tool your prospects interact with directly. You send them one link — they go through a guided 2-minute benefits assessment, book a virtual consultation with you, and sign a consultation authorization, all without you having to be involved in real time.

💬

Conversational Flow

Feels like a chat, not a form. Prospects answer questions one at a time in a natural back-and-forth that keeps them engaged and moving forward.

📅

Books the Appointment

A Calendly window opens inside the flow. Prospects pick a time that works for them — and they can't move forward until they actually complete the booking.

✍

Captures a Signed SOA

After booking, prospects sign a Scope of Appointment authorization directly in the widget. You get a warm, pre-qualified lead who's already committed to a call.

📱

Alerts You Instantly

The moment a prospect books an appointment through Calendly, you receive a text message with their name, email, appointment time, and the meeting join link.

🔗

What Your Prospect Sees

The complete assessment — their view

Here's exactly what happens when someone opens your widget link. The whole thing takes about 2 minutes.

1

Friendly intro from you

The bot greets them with your name and agency, and explains that you help employers offer financially predictable, cost-controlled benefits while giving employees access to better, more affordable coverage. Asks if they have a couple minutes.

2

Company & role questions

They type their company name, select their role (Owner, HR, CFO, etc.), and choose how many employees the company has.

3

Current benefits situation

Do they currently offer benefits? Simple yes/no choice that helps you understand where they're starting from.

4

Benefits interests (multi-select)

They check all the benefits they're interested in — health, dental, vision, life insurance, ICHRA, voluntary benefits. They can select as many as apply.

🆕 If they select ICHRA, 4 additional questions appear specific to ICHRA — budget, employee count, timeline, and current challenges. You'll have deep info before the call.
5

Timeline & contact info

When are they looking to implement? Then their phone number (auto-formats as they type) and business email.

6

Book a virtual appointment

Your Calendly scheduling window opens. They pick a date and time that works for them. The continue button is locked until Calendly confirms the booking — they cannot skip this step.

7

Sign the consultation authorization

A digital signature pad appears. They sign their name with their finger or mouse to confirm they've requested a consultation. This is your Scope of Appointment.

✓

You're All Set!

The confirmation screen shows them your name, direct phone number, and email so they know exactly who to expect a call from and how to reach you.

📣 Marketing Tools

Pre-written outreach templates (email, text, LinkedIn), your prospect list with message tracking, and social media post templates — all with your widget link built in.

1

How to use Marketing Templates

Go to Marketing → Templates tab. Type the prospect's first name and company in the fields at the top. Click any template to preview it with their name filled in. Click Add Widget Link — then Copy Message. Paste directly into your text or email.

✅ You must click "Add Widget Link" before the Copy button activates. This ensures you never accidentally send a message without your booking link.
2

How to use the Prospect List

Go to Marketing → Prospect List tab. If you have a spreadsheet of contacts, save it as a CSV (Name, Company, Phone, Email columns) and upload it. Then select a prospect from the dropdown, choose a message type, add widget link, and copy. The system logs every message you send so you never double-message someone.

3

How to use Social Media Posts

Go to Marketing → Social Media tab. Your profile links are saved there. On the right, you'll see a ready-made post for each platform. Click Add Widget Link, then Copy Post — paste directly into LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, or X.

Tips for Success

Getting the most out of the widget

💬

Send with context

A personalized text or email before the link performs far better than a cold link drop. Use the Marketing templates — they're written to get opens and clicks.

🕐

Follow up fast

When you get the appointment text alert, confirm in your own calendar right away. Prospects who book through the widget are warm — don't let momentum cool off.

📋

Review before every call

Check their Lead entry in the dashboard before the consultation. Their answers tell you exactly what to focus on — you'll sound like you've known them for weeks.

✊

Don't pitch — listen

The widget already pre-qualifies them. By the time you're on a call, they've self-identified their needs. Your job is to listen, confirm, and present options — not sell from scratch.

📧

Add it to your signature

Every email you send is an opportunity. A simple "See if we're a fit — 2 minutes" with your widget link at the bottom of every email works quietly in the background.

👥

Use your prospect list

Upload your existing contacts as a CSV into the Prospect List tab. Work through them systematically — the tracker shows who you've messaged, when, and with what type of outreach.
