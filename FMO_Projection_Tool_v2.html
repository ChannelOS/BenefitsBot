Hierarchy Levels Explanation

TVP TVP (DVP)

Distribution Vice President — one per region. Top of hierarchy. 5 total.

MKD MKD (Market Director)

Market Director — multiple per region, one per territory. Reports to TVP. 52 total.

RSC RSC (Regional Sales Coordinator)

Regional Sales Coordinator — multiple per territory. Reports to MKD. 300 total.

DSC DSC (District Sales Coordinator)

District Sales Coordinator — reports to RSC. ~4 per RSC. 1,200 total.

Asc Asc (Associate)

Associate — enters projections. Reports to DSC. ~10 per DSC. 12,000 total.

Writing Number Format

T-10114 TVP (5 people): T-1 + 4-digit sequential ID
M-20001 MKD (52 people): M-2 + 4-digit sequential ID
R-30001 RSC (300 people): R-3 + 4-digit sequential ID
D-40001 DSC (1,200 people): D-4 + 4-digit sequential ID
A-50001 Asc (12,000 people): A-5 + 4-digit sequential ID
